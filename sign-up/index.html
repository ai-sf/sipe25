Sign up

General informations and deadlines

Registrations will start on the 3rd of June at 10:00 (GMT+2) and will last at least until the 20th of June at 23:59 (GMT+2).
Following the registration period, we expect to email all applicants by the beginning of July. Payments will then be required (by bank transfer or PayPal) within 15 days of receiving an acceptance email from the organising committee.
Please read the terms and conditions below for further clarifications and note that, upon registration, you will be asked for personal details (such as name, surname, date of birth, university affiliation, etc.).

Participation fee, requirements and selection

In order to enroll in the event you must be an AISF or RSEF member.
Note: if you are Italian and you are not an AISF member yet, you can become one at this link.
Note: if you are Spanish and you are not a Grupo de Estudiantes RSEF member, you can become one at this link.

The selected candidates will have to cover a participation fee of
• €100 if they choose to stay in the chosen facility of Hotel Campus;
• €50 if the candidates prefer to not use our facilities.

Such fee will cover:
• accommodation in Bari;
• coffee breaks, lunches and dinners included in the program;
• all breakfasts;
• transportation to/from Polignano and to/from Valenzano;
• access to the various facilities and events (university, laboratories, conferences);
• welcome bag with themed gadgets.

Please notice that participation fees does not cover the following:
• journeys to arrive at the event in Bari and leave once it is concluded;
• lunches and dinners not included in the programm;
• insurance.

Parallel sessions and Talks

During the event a set of parallel sessions will take place, so everyone is invited to apply and present their current research work, whether it is part of a thesis project (at bachelor, master or PhD level) or is an outcome of a summer programme or personal project.

Important notes

• Possible changes: the program will be constantly updated as further details are provided;
• Meeting time: It would be best to arrive in Bari on October 2nd by 1:00-2:00 PM , to attend the event’s opening ceremony. If you are unable to arrive within this timeframe, please inform us via email or IG a few days before the event.
• Meeting point: The meeting point (where the event organizers will welcome you) will be communicated via email to participants and on the SIPE25 Telegram group (the link to the Telegram channel will be sent via email to participants).
