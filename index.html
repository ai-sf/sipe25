“Fisica senza confini, Física sin fronteras”

What does this mean?

We believe that Physics can and must spread throughout the world.
But how can a physicist help nowadays?
There are various possibilities, ranging from research to working in industry, and even science communication.
At SIPE25, we want to explore each of these opportunities in order to talk about Physics without borders.

Welcome to Bari

Located on the Adriatic coast, Bari is a vibrant city rich in history, culture, and hospitality. As the host of this exchange program, Bari offers the perfect setting for learning, connection, and discovery.

Are you ready for SIPE25?

This international program brings together physics students for a short but intensive experience of learning and cultural exchange. Participants will attend lectures by distinguished scientists, take part in social events, and explore key research and cultural sites across Bari.
